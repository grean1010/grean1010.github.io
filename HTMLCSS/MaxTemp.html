As expected, daily maximum temperatures were lowest further from the equator (latitude = 0). Because temperature data were taken during summer in the northern hemisphere we see many higher temperatures in the lower, positive latitudes than in the southern hemisphere. There were some surprises, low temperatures near the equator, for example that were due to cities at higher elevations.
